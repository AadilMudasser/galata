Our Story

Craft, kept
deliberately small.

Galata began as a single chair in Istanbul's old quarter, where a barber's reputation travelled by word alone. That same discipline now lives inside a private townhouse in Marsa — low light, slow hands, and nothing on the walls that doesn't earn its place.

Every appointment opens with a short consultation, because no two heads of hair — or beards, or moods — call for the same approach. We work in wool towels, straight razors and silence when it's wanted, conversation when it isn't.
